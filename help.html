Instructions

Every new race generates a unique track! Use the arrow keys for acceleration, braking and steering. The game ends when you complete 5 laps.

Scoring

Finishing in 1st place gains 5 points, 2nd place gains 4 points etc. You must finish in the top 3 to advance to the next race. If you don't, GAME OVER!
